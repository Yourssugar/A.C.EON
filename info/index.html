ARCHIVE RULES
What this site is, why it exists, and how to interact with it.

01 WHAT THIS IS

A hidden companion site for the A.C.EON channel — an archive that grows piece by piece. Some content is open to everyone, some is locked behind codes you have to find.

02 HOW HIDDEN CODES WORK

Hidden codes are tucked into frames, descriptions, track spectrograms, and other places connected to the channel's uploads. One code unlocks one section of the archive.

Found a code? Enter it into the terminal on the home page.

03 PUBLIC CODES

A small set of codes — like GAME and HELLO — are public from the start. No searching needed. It's a way to give visitors something right away, with more to come over time.

04 PRIVACY

This site doesn't collect any visitor data. Anything saved (themes, game high scores, progress) stays in your own browser's local storage and is never sent anywhere.

05 WHAT'S NEXT

The archive grows alongside the channel: new videos, new hidden codes, new sections. Follow A.C.EON's uploads if you want to be among the first to unlock the next fragment.
